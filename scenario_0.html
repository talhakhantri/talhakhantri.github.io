Bring your ideas to life:

This tool lets you turn your ideas into pictures. Here's how to use it:
  1. Type a description of the picture you're thinking of in the box below.
  2. Click "Generate Images".
  3. Wait about 20 seconds for the pictures to show up. You should see five pictures.
  4. Drag each picture in the slots below to rank it. The one that matches your idea the best should go in the first slot on the left, under "Best".
  5. When you're done assigning ranks, click "Submit Ranks".
You can change your description and make new generate images as many times as you want. But remember, you can only submit your rankings once.
